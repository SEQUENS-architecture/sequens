About the new registers

England and Wales are introducing Children Not in School registers. The law passed in April 2026, but start dates are not yet set and nothing is required of you today. Home education remains lawful, and the National Curriculum remains optional.

When the registers begin, the record you already keep here is what you would draw on. No extra work, no panic.

Built on the same evidence as the school engine.

The home education planner runs on the SEQUENS engine, and the engine is built on this research. Open a finding:

Little and often beats one big push.+

Memory is strengthened by being used. Short retrieval, spaced out over time, keeps learning alive far better than covering something once and moving on.

SEQUENS builds in little and often: the map tracks when something was secured and gently resurfaces it when a revisit is due, as a five-minute practice, not a test.
Roediger & Karpicke · spacing-effect research · EEF
We think in words and pictures, so learning uses both.+

A clear explanation beside a simple, matched picture lets more be understood and more be kept than either alone, for every child, and especially for children still growing into English.

SEQUENS is dual coded: Frames put a picture beside the thinking in every session, and symbol support is there for the children who need a way in.
Paivio, dual coding · Mayer, multimedia learning
One thing at a time, pitched right, is how minds work.+

Working memory is small. A session that asks for one clear thing, entered at your child's level, will always beat a crowded worksheet.

SEQUENS does one thing at a time: every session follows the six-part shape, one focus, one shared task, with support and depth shaped around your actual child.
Cognitive-load theory · Sweller · EEF
Inclusion is a foundation, not a feature.+

Reaching every child, including children with additional needs and children learning English, is the condition on the whole design, not an option beside it.

SEQUENS is inclusive by design: reading level, chunked instructions, symbol support and interest-shaped practice are set once on your child's profile and applied to every session after.
EEF SEND guidance · dual-coding research · the SEQUENS engine, adaptive by default
