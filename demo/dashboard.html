Eleven lessons built, decks and worksheets included. You reviewed and changed four of them, which is the point.
What is deliberately not here. No league table of your class against another. No performance score attached to your name. No pupil sees any of this. A dashboard that can be used to judge a teacher stops being used honestly by that teacher.

It marks by exception

Twenty-six children needed nothing from you today, so they are not on the screen. Four are, with the reason next to the name. That is the whole marking screen.

It reports understanding, not completion

Nine wrong answers is not a percentage. It is a named misconception, in the words the children actually chose, because the hinge was built to reveal exactly that.

It suggests, you confirm

Every recommendation shows its reasoning and waits. Nothing moves in the tracker, the plan or a child's record until a human presses the button.

Honest about what this is. This is a mockup, drawn to show the shape of the thing. The numbers are illustrative and the children are invented. SEQUENS is in pilot and there are no impact data yet, because nobody has run it. The time-saved figure is what the engine would account for, not a claim we have measured in a school.
